Global sustainability



We’re all connected to sustainability














At FIS®, we know that making a difference with environmental, social and corporate governance (ESG) is a team effort. We're leveraging our position as a world fintech leader to advance sustainability in three key areas: fostering inclusive and empowered societies, protecting our planet, and driving governance of responsible and sustainable growth. Review our report for a closer look at how we’re prioritizing ESG.

Our position, 
our promise


Trust is a powerful resource.


Every day, individuals and institutions worldwide entrust us with their financial futures. We’re working to grow that confidence because their successes have been and will continue to be the measure of ours. From a perspective that spans the globe, we understand the inseparable bond between commerce and community and just how deeply we’re all connected. With a commitment to doing more than what’s right, today we’re making decisions for a better tomorrow.


Driven by our core values, we uphold inclusivity, philanthropy and environmental sustainability. Through ESG, we hold ourselves accountable, guarding the trust of our clients, colleagues and partners, and doing our part to achieve a future that we can all enjoy.


Because when the world thrives, we’re doing our best work.


That’s the power of FIS.










































"Every day, FIS helps power the financial technology that economies rely on, businesses run on and innovators build on. We take this responsibility seriously and are committed to growing our company in a sustainable, responsible manner. We are passionate about our core value to “Be the Change” for our clients, colleagues and communities."


 


Stephanie Ferris 

 CEO and President, FIS
