Building the Future: Our Report




The NTT DATA Sustainability Report provides comprehensive insights into the company’s annual performance in sustainability, highlighting the environmental, social, and governance impacts, risks, and opportunities that are relevant to our business. This report, reviewed and approved by the Board of Directors, has been prepared in accordance with the requirements of Spanish Law 11/2018 on non-financial and diversity information. It is also voluntarily and partially aligned with the structure and content of the European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS).
